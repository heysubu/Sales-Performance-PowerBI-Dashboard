# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard — Power BI Case Study

A professional **Power BI dashboard** that shows how a business is performing in sales. It helps managers and business owners see their sales numbers, taxes, and city-wise performance — all in one place, in a clear and simple way.

---

## 📋 What This Does

Many businesses have lots of sales data but no easy way to read it. This dashboard solves that problem.

**With this Power BI report, you can:**
- See total sales and tax numbers at a glance
- Track how sales changed every quarter from 2018 to 2020
- Find out which cities are selling the most
- Filter data by state or date range
- View sales locations on a live world map

## ✨ Main Features

1. **3 KPI Cards at the Top** — Shows Total Taxes (14M), Sales Excluding Taxes (92M), and Sales Including Taxes (106M) in big bold numbers. Easy to read at a glance!

2. **Sales By Quarter Chart** — A smooth area chart showing how sales and taxes moved from January 2018 to December 2020. See the full trend clearly.

3. **Sales by City Bar Chart** — Shows which cities made the most sales. Top cities like Lisco and Orchard Mesa stand out at 30M+ each.

4. **Interactive World Map** — All sales locations shown on a real map. Bigger dots = more sales in that city.

5. **Data Table with Details** — Full table showing Year, Quarter, Sales (Inc. Taxes), Sales (Exc. Taxes), and Tax Amount for every period.

6. **Two Smart Slicers (Filters)** — Filter by **State/Province Name** or **Date Range** to zoom into exactly what you need.

7. **Clean Dark Theme Design** — Professional design with clear fonts. Easy to read on screen or in presentations.

## 🚀 How to Use

**Step 1 — Open the file**
Download the `.pbix` file and open it in **Power BI Desktop** (free software from Microsoft).

**Step 2 — Explore the dashboard**
You will see the "Sales Reports" page. All charts and numbers load automatically.

**Step 3 — Use the filters**
Click the **StateProvinceName** dropdown to filter by state. Use the **Date slider** to zoom into a specific time period and all charts update instantly.

## 🔧 How to Customize

Want to connect your own data?

- Open Power BI Desktop → Click **Transform Data**
- Replace the sample data with your own sales table
- Make sure your table has: Date, City, Sales Amount, Tax Amount columns
- Refresh and your dashboard updates automatically!
